Tailored Capital Structure for Large Commercial Ventures

Large infrastructure works, real estate township developments, industrial factories, and warehouse structures need specialized funding. These requirements cannot be processed via standard retail banking channels. They require structured project reports, CMA (Credit Monitoring Arrangement) data arrays, and underwriter board review presentations.

MLG Finedge acts as your corporate financial consultant. We help structure your debt packages, formulate credit reports, check land margins, compile government approvals, and present dossiers to lead bankers and consortium groups.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum funding: ₹10 Crores
  • Reputed builder / corporate promoter
  • Valid land approvals (registry, NA status)
  • Feasibility studies & CMA summaries

Documents Required

  • Detailed Project Report (DPR)
  • 3 Years audited corporate balance sheets
  • Land registry title chains & maps
  • Municipal blueprint map approvals
