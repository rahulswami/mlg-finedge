Working Capital Loan (OD/CC)

Rate: Starting @ 10.50% p.a.
Type: Cash Credit & Overdraft Limits
Tenure: 1 Year renewable

Optimize Cash Flow with Overdraft and Cash Credit Lines

Working capital facilities are structured specifically to balance operational cash flow, buy inventory, pay vendor bills, and finance accounts receivables (debtors lists). Rather than a term loan where you pay interest on the full amount, OD/CC lines charge interest only on the daily utilized balance.

At MLG Finedge, we evaluate your drawing power based on active stock, aging debtors registers, and business sales velocity to help you secure high-limit cash credits from leading banks at minimum margins.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Annual turnover: ₹50 Lakhs min
  • GST active registration: 1 Year min
  • Clean banking transactions
  • Audited financials for corporate cases

Documents Required

  • Promoter KYC & Business PAN
  • Last 12 Months GST returns
  • 12 Months main banking statement
  • Current stock & debtors report
